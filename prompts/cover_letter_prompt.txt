Write a compelling cover letter for {candidate_name} applying to {job_title} at {company_name}.

Context:
Company brief: {company_brief}
Tailored resume: {tailored_resume}
Job responsibilities: {responsibilities}

Structure:
- Paragraph 1 (Hook): Why this specific company excites the candidate — reference something from the company brief.
- Paragraph 2 (Fit): Connect 2-3 of the candidate's strongest experiences directly to the JD's key responsibilities.
- Paragraph 3 (Close): Express enthusiasm, mention availability for an interview, confident sign-off.

Tone: Professional but not stiff, confident but not arrogant, concise (under 350 words).
Reference the company by name at least twice. Do not use generic phrases. Start with something specific and engaging.
